Spotify Social Feature

Period: February-March 2018
Project for the course in Interaction Design Methods (DH2628) at KTH.
The goal of the course was to learn user-centered design. Our final project was to look at music consumption and investigate what can be improved. Finally, do a redesign of some aspects of our chosen solution, app, service. My group decided to focus on exploring the social aspect of music and how Spotify can connect people through music.

about me

a very short introduction

I'm currently at my final year of studies at KTH, doing a Master's in Media Management/Technology. I'm looking for thesis work within UX design in order to develop my interests in this area and hopefully land a job in this field in the future.

Except for studying, I've also been working part-time within the e-commerce department at a Swedish brand called Happy Socks. Me and my team are responsible for the Customer Experience which means finding and trying new ways of how to improve the overall shopping experience on the Happy Socks'website.

My main interests which I try to develop in my free time are: coding, UX/UI design, graphic design and online marketing.
